If you have JAVA heap space recurrent error in Topspin, it is because of the xml files generated.

Do the following:
1. Close Topspin.
2. Go to /opt/topspinXX/prog/curdir/nmr/db/local/
3. Rename folder pp to pp_old.
4. Restart Topspin.
